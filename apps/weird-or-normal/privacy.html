Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Weird or Normal collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application.
Last updated: May 28, 2025

1. Who We Are

Weird or Normal is operated by the Weird or Normal team. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

2. Age Requirement

The App is intended for users aged 12 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under the age of 12.

3. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our services, we may collect the following information:

  • Basic account information, such as name and email address
  • Profile information provided by you, including profile photo and bio
  • Content you create, including questions, votes, and comments
  • App usage data and interaction data
  • Device and technical information, such as device type and OS version

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Display user-generated content and profiles
  • Enable interaction between users
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve features, performance, and user experience
  • Ensure safety, moderation, and compliance with our rules

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data only with:

  • Service providers, such as hosting, storage, analytics, and moderation tools
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights and users

6. Advertising

The App may display advertisements. Ads may be based on limited, anonymized, or contextual data. You can manage ad preferences through your device settings where applicable.

7. Cookies and Device Data

We may use cookies or similar technologies to support app functionality, analytics, and performance. You can control permissions through your device settings.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable period, unless retention is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Please keep your login credentials confidential.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

You can exercise these rights by contacting us.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time by contacting us at friendsphere.app@icloud.com.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 12 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a user under 12, we will delete such data promptly.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated within the App.

13. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can contact us at:

friendsphere.app@icloud.com
